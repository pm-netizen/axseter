Structured Cabling Installation

Enterprise-grade cabling infrastructure deployed nationwide with a Single Point of Contact. Cat6, Cat6A, and fiber optic solutions for multi-location organizations.

The Challenge

Managing Cabling Across Dozens of Locations Is a Headache

When you're opening 50, 100, or 500+ locations per year, structured cabling becomes a coordination nightmare. Different contractors in every market. Inconsistent quality. Competing standards. And you're left playing project manager for dozens of vendors who have never worked together.

The result? Cost overruns averaging 15-25%, timeline delays that push back openings, and an ongoing support mess when something breaks six months later.

Axseter eliminates that fragmentation. One partner. One standard. Every location.

Common Questions

What is a Single Point of Contact (SPoC) model for cabling?

A SPoC model means you work with one company‚ÄîAxseter‚Äîfor all your nationwide cabling needs instead of managing dozens of regional contractors. We handle technician dispatch, quality control, and project coordination, giving you one invoice, one relationship, and consistent quality across all locations.

How long does structured cabling installation take per location?

Installation timelines depend on location size and complexity. A typical 2,000-5,000 sq ft office takes 1-2 days. Larger facilities of 10,000+ sq ft may require 3-5 days. We coordinate with your construction schedules and can deploy multiple locations simultaneously.

What cabling standards does Axseter follow?

All Axseter installations follow BICSI, TIA/EIA, and NEC standards. We provide certified test results for every cable run and offer warranties on workmanship and materials. Our technicians hold BICSI certifications and maintain ongoing training.

Can Axseter handle rapid multi-location rollouts?

Yes. Axseter specializes in high-velocity deployments for companies opening 50-500+ locations per year. Our national technician network and centralized dispatch system allow us to deploy structured cabling across multiple locations simultaneously while maintaining consistent quality.

What areas does Axseter cover?

Axseter provides structured cabling installation services across all 50 states. Our headquarters are in Chicago with regional offices in Indianapolis and Atlanta, but our technician network enables nationwide coverage.
