Access Control Installation

Enterprise-grade access control systems deployed nationwide. One contractor, consistent quality, unified security across every location.

The Multi-Location Access Control Challenge

For organizations managing dozens or hundreds of locations, access control presents a unique operational challenge. Each facility needs secure, reliable entry systems—but coordinating installation, maintenance, and management across a distributed portfolio creates complexity that compounds with scale.

Many enterprise clients come to Axseter facing a familiar situation: inconsistent access control platforms inherited through acquisitions, aging systems that don't integrate with modern management tools, or simply the operational burden of managing relationships with dozens of regional security contractors.

The result is typically a fragmented security posture—different credential types at different locations, no centralized visibility into access events, and unpredictable costs when systems need service. For PE-backed companies focused on operational efficiency or publicly traded firms with compliance obligations, this fragmentation creates real business risk.

Axseter's Approach to Enterprise Access Control

Axseter brings the same Single Point of Contact model that's transformed how enterprise clients manage structured cabling to the access control domain. We serve as your nationwide installation and service partner, providing consistent deployment quality whether you're securing a clinic in Phoenix or a branch office in Boston.

Our approach starts with understanding your security requirements, compliance obligations, and operational workflows. We work with your security team, IT department, and facilities managers to design standardized access control specifications that can be replicated across your entire portfolio. This standardization is critical—it means every new location deploys the same hardware configurations, follows the same installation procedures, and integrates seamlessly with your central management platform.

From there, we execute. Our nationwide technician network deploys access control systems in coordination with your construction schedules, whether you're opening 10 locations this quarter or 200 this year. Every installation follows your approved specifications, includes complete documentation, and undergoes quality verification before handoff.

Access Control Technologies We Deploy

Card Reader Systems

Proximity cards, smart cards, and mobile credentials form the foundation of most commercial access control deployments. Axseter installs readers from all major manufacturers including HID, ASSA ABLOY, and Allegion. We handle the complete installation scope: readers, controllers, door hardware, power supplies, and integration with your chosen access control platform.

Biometric Access Systems

For higher-security applications, biometric authentication provides an additional layer of verification. We deploy fingerprint readers, facial recognition systems, and multi-factor authentication solutions that combine biometrics with card or PIN credentials. These systems are increasingly common in healthcare settings with medication storage requirements or financial services locations with vault access.

Cloud-Based Access Control

Modern cloud-based platforms like Verkada, Openpath, and Brivo have transformed enterprise access control management. These systems provide centralized visibility across all locations, remote credential management, and integration with other building systems. Axseter is certified to install and configure these platforms, ensuring your cloud deployment is optimized from day one.

Visitor Management Systems

Enterprise visitor management goes beyond a paper logbook. We deploy integrated visitor management kiosks that pre-register guests, print temporary badges, notify hosts of arrivals, and maintain audit trails for compliance. These systems integrate with your access control platform to issue time-limited credentials and track visitor movements throughout your facilities.

Door Hardware and Electric Locks

Access control is only as good as the door hardware it controls. Axseter installs electric strikes, magnetic locks, electrified panic hardware, and automatic door operators. We coordinate with your architectural hardware specifications and ensure all installations meet fire code and ADA requirements.

Industry-Specific Access Control Solutions

Healthcare and Medtail

Healthcare facilities face unique access control requirements driven by HIPAA, Joint Commission standards, and controlled substance regulations. Axseter deploys access control solutions that address medication room security, after-hours access management, and patient area protection. We understand the workflow requirements of clinical environments and design systems that secure facilities without impeding patient care.

Financial Services

Banks, insurance offices, and financial services firms require access control that addresses both physical security and regulatory compliance. We deploy systems with robust audit trails, integration with intrusion detection, and vault-grade security for sensitive areas. Our installations support the dual-control and time-delay requirements common in financial environments.

Retail

Multi-location retailers need access control that balances security with operational flexibility. We deploy systems that manage employee access across shifts, secure stockrooms and cash offices, and integrate with loss prevention strategies. For retailers with high employee turnover, we emphasize platforms with efficient credential management workflows.

Our Process

1. Assessment and Design

We begin with a thorough understanding of your security requirements, compliance obligations, and operational workflows. For clients with existing installations, we audit current systems to inform standardization recommendations. The output is a detailed specification package that defines hardware, installation standards, and integration requirements.

2. Standardization

We create deployment packages tailored to your location types. A 3,000 square foot clinic has different access control needs than a 15,000 square foot regional office. We define standard configurations for each location type, streamlining procurement and installation while ensuring appropriate security coverage.

3. Deployment

Our project managers coordinate installations with your construction schedules and internal stakeholders. We handle permitting where required, coordinate with general contractors and other trades, and ensure installations don't disrupt ongoing operations at existing locations.

4. Commissioning and Training

Every installation includes system commissioning—verifying that all doors, readers, and credentials function correctly. We provide documentation packages and training for local staff on daily operations. Integration with your central management platform is verified before project closeout.

5. Ongoing Support

Post-installation, Axseter provides break-fix support with defined SLAs. Our technicians can respond to hardware failures, assist with adds/moves/changes, and support planned maintenance activities across your portfolio.

Common Questions

What access control platforms does Axseter install?

Axseter is platform-agnostic and installs all major commercial access control systems including Verkada, Openpath, Brivo, HID, Genetec, S2, Lenel, and many others. We work with your security team to determine the best platform for your needs or install your specified system across all locations.

Can you standardize access control across our existing locations?

Yes. Many clients come to us with inconsistent access control systems across locations acquired through M&A or organic growth. We audit your current infrastructure, recommend a standardization strategy, and execute the rollout—whether that's upgrading legacy systems or deploying a unified cloud-based platform.

How does Axseter handle access control for rapid expansion?

Our SPoC model is built for velocity. We create standardized deployment packages for your location types, pre-stage equipment, and coordinate with your construction schedules. We've supported clients opening 200+ locations per year with consistent access control deployments.

Does Axseter provide ongoing support for access control systems?

Yes. Beyond installation, we offer break-fix support, preventive maintenance, and system administration assistance. Our nationwide technician network means we can dispatch for repairs or adds/moves/changes at any of your locations within standard SLA windows.

How do you handle integration with existing security systems?

Access control rarely exists in isolation. We coordinate integration with video surveillance, intrusion detection, and building management systems. Our technicians understand the integration protocols and can ensure your access control platform communicates properly with other security infrastructure.

What compliance standards do your installations meet?

Axseter installations comply with relevant codes and standards including NFPA, ADA, and local fire codes. For industry-specific requirements like HIPAA or PCI-DSS, we work with your compliance team to ensure installations meet your security policy requirements.
