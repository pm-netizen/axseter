Audio-Visual Installation

Enterprise AV systems deployed nationwide. Conference rooms, digital signage, and presentation technology with consistent quality across every location.

The Enterprise AV Challenge

In today's distributed workplace, audio-visual technology is critical infrastructure. Conference rooms need reliable video conferencing for hybrid meetings. Lobbies require digital signage that reinforces your brand. Training rooms demand presentation systems that support effective learning. And across all these applications, consistency matters—your team in Denver should have the same meeting experience as your team in Boston.

For organizations with dozens or hundreds of locations, achieving this consistency is challenging. Different regional AV contractors install different equipment, configure systems differently, and deliver varying levels of quality. The result is a fragmented technology experience that frustrates users and creates support headaches for IT teams.

Axseter brings the same Single Point of Contact discipline that enterprises rely on for cabling and security to the audio-visual domain. We deploy standardized AV systems nationwide, ensuring consistent equipment, configuration, and user experience across your entire location portfolio.

Axseter's AV Approach

Our approach begins with understanding how your organization uses AV technology. Conference rooms have different requirements than training facilities. Executive boardrooms need different capabilities than small huddle spaces. Digital signage in a retail environment serves different purposes than lobby displays in a professional services firm.

We work with your IT and facilities teams to develop standardized deployment packages for each room type. These packages define equipment specifications, mounting standards, cable management requirements, and system configurations. The standardization ensures every location receives a consistent installation while allowing appropriate customization for room-specific needs.

From there, our nationwide technician network executes deployments in coordination with your construction schedules. Whether you're outfitting a new office or refreshing conference rooms across 50 locations, you work with a single Axseter project manager who coordinates the entire program.

AV Technologies We Deploy

Conference Room Systems

Modern conference rooms require integrated systems that make video conferencing seamless. Axseter installs complete conference room solutions including displays, cameras, microphones, speakers, and room control interfaces. We work with all major platforms—Microsoft Teams, Zoom, Cisco Webex, Google Meet—ensuring your rooms are certified and optimized for your chosen platform.

For Microsoft Teams environments, we deploy Teams Rooms systems from Poly, Logitech, Yealink, and other certified manufacturers. For Zoom, we install Zoom Rooms hardware. Platform-agnostic deployments using bring-your-own-device (BYOD) configurations are also available for organizations that want flexibility.

Huddle Spaces

Small meeting spaces of 2-4 people need simple, reliable technology. We deploy compact all-in-one systems or streamlined camera-display combinations that minimize complexity while delivering excellent video and audio quality. These spaces typically use USB-connected peripherals with laptop-based conferencing.

Boardrooms and Large Conference Rooms

Executive boardrooms and large conference rooms require more sophisticated systems. We deploy multiple displays or video walls, ceiling microphone arrays, PTZ cameras, and professional audio systems. Room control panels provide one-touch meeting start and intuitive source selection.

Digital Signage

Digital signage communicates with employees, visitors, and customers throughout your facilities. Axseter installs commercial-grade displays, media players, and mounting systems for lobby directories, wayfinding, menu boards, promotional displays, and internal communications. We ensure proper connectivity to your content management system.

Video Walls

For high-impact visual experiences, video walls create immersive displays in lobbies, conference rooms, and public spaces. We install LED video walls and multi-panel LCD configurations with the processing hardware needed to drive content across the array.

Training and Presentation Rooms

Training facilities need flexible presentation capabilities—wireless screen sharing, document cameras, recording systems, and audience microphones. We install systems per your AV specifications that support various training scenarios from instructor-led sessions to interactive workshops.

Background Music and Paging

Commercial audio systems provide background music, paging, and announcements throughout your facilities. We install distributed speaker systems with appropriate zoning and integration with paging sources.

The Standardization Advantage

For multi-location organizations, AV standardization delivers significant benefits:

Consistent User Experience

When every conference room works the same way, employees can walk into any location and immediately start a meeting. No learning curve, no fumbling with unfamiliar equipment—just consistent, reliable technology.

Simplified Support

Standardized equipment means your IT helpdesk can troubleshoot any room using the same procedures. Spare parts inventory is simplified. Training materials apply across all locations.

Volume Purchasing Power

Committing to standard equipment configurations enables volume pricing from manufacturers and distributors. The savings can be significant across a large deployment.

Faster Deployments

With standardized packages already defined, new location buildouts move faster. Equipment is pre-configured before it ships. Installation procedures are documented and repeatable.

Our Process

1. Scope Review & Planning

We begin by reviewing your AV specifications—what platforms you use, what room types you have, and your standard configurations. Site visits or floor plan reviews help us understand physical constraints and coordinate installation logistics.

2. Coordination

We align our teams with your deployment packages for each room type. A small huddle space gets one configuration, a medium conference room gets another, and executive boardrooms get premium treatment. We ensure consistent execution of your defined equipment and installation standards.

3. Installation

Our technicians install displays, mount cameras and microphones, run necessary cabling, and rack equipment. We coordinate with construction schedules and other trades to ensure installations happen at the optimal time.

4. Configuration and Testing

Systems are configured to your specifications—platform settings, room names, calling configurations, and integrations with room scheduling systems. Every system is tested to verify audio and video quality before handoff.

5. Training and Documentation

We provide end-user quick reference guides and administrator documentation. Training sessions ensure your facilities and IT teams can support day-to-day operations and basic troubleshooting.

6. Ongoing Support

Post-installation support includes break-fix service for hardware issues and assistance with configuration changes. Our nationwide coverage means we can respond to issues at any location.

FAQ

Common Questions

What AV platforms does Axseter install?

Axseter installs conference room systems for all major platforms including Microsoft Teams, Zoom, Cisco Webex, and Google Meet. For digital signage, we work with platforms like BrightSign, Scala, and Four Winds. We're platform-agnostic and deploy the systems specified by your IT team or help evaluate options.

Can you standardize conference rooms across all our locations?

Yes. Standardization is one of the key benefits of working with Axseter. We develop deployment packages for your conference room types—small huddle spaces, medium conference rooms, and large boardrooms. Every location receives the same equipment, configuration, and user experience.

Does Axseter handle digital signage content management?

We focus on the physical installation—displays, media players, mounts, and connectivity. For content management, we work with your marketing team or their agency to ensure the signage system is properly configured to receive content from your chosen CMS platform.

How long does a typical conference room installation take?

A standard conference room installation typically takes 1-2 days depending on complexity. This includes display mounting, camera and microphone installation, cabling, and system configuration. We coordinate with your schedules to minimize disruption to room availability.

Do you provide training for end users?

Yes. We provide quick reference guides and can conduct training sessions for facilities staff or power users. The goal is to ensure your team can effectively use the systems and handle basic troubleshooting.

What about ongoing maintenance and support?

Axseter offers break-fix support for AV systems. When hardware fails or systems need service, our technicians can respond at any of your locations. We also assist with firmware updates, configuration changes, and system optimization.
