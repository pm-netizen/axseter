The Challenge

Scaling Infrastructure Across a National Footprint

ATI Physical Therapy operates one of the largest networks of outpatient physical therapy clinics in the United States. With over 900 locations across 48 states, their facilities team faced significant challenges managing low voltage infrastructure at scale.

Each new clinic opening required coordination with local contractors who had no institutional knowledge of ATI's standards. Break-fix issues meant tracking down whoever originally installed the system—often a different contractor at each location. Technology refresh programs required project managing dozens of vendors simultaneously.

ATI needed a partner who could deliver consistent quality across their entire footprint while simplifying vendor management and ensuring reliable ongoing support.

The Solution

A Single Partner for All Infrastructure Needs

New Clinic Construction

Axseter handles all low voltage installation for new ATI clinics—structured cabling, access control, video surveillance, and AV systems. Our standardized specifications ensure every clinic is built to the same standards, regardless of location.

Break-Fix Support

When infrastructure issues arise at any ATI location, one call dispatches a qualified technician. We already know ATI's systems, so troubleshooting is faster and resolution rates are higher.

Technology Refresh

As ATI updates technology standards across their network, Axseter executes the rollout. Coordinated deployments across multiple locations ensure consistent upgrades without disrupting clinic operations.

The Results

Operational Excellence at Scale

Simplified Operations

One vendor relationship replaces dozens of regional contractors. ATI's facilities team manages projects, not vendor logistics.

Predictable Service Levels

Consistent response times and resolution rates across all locations, with SLAs that ATI can count on.

Consistent Quality

Every clinic built to the same standards means IT can troubleshoot remotely and spare parts are interchangeable.

Cost Visibility

Transparent pricing with clear cost drivers. No surprise invoices from unfamiliar contractors.

Reduced Admin Burden

One contract, one invoice process, one point of contact for all infrastructure needs nationwide.

Scalable Partnership

As ATI grows, Axseter scales with them—no need to rebuild vendor relationships in new markets.
