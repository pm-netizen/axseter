The Challenge

High-Velocity Healthcare Expansion

Oak Street Health operates a network of value-based primary care centers focused on Medicare patients. Their care model requires purpose-built facilities with specific infrastructure requirements—from EMR connectivity to patient check-in systems to telehealth capabilities.

With an aggressive expansion strategy opening 50+ new centers annually, Oak Street needed an infrastructure partner who could match their velocity without compromising on quality. Each new center required identical infrastructure to support their standardized care delivery model.

The alternative—coordinating with different contractors in each market—would create quality variations, slow down openings, and generate ongoing support headaches as the network grew.

The Solution

Standardized Deployment at Scale

New Center Buildouts

Complete low voltage installation for each new Oak Street center—structured cabling for EMR workstations, access control for patient and staff areas, video surveillance, and AV systems for telehealth.

Standardized Specifications

Every center built to identical standards. Same cable labeling, same rack layouts, same equipment configurations. Oak Street's IT team can support any center remotely because they're all the same.

Ongoing Support

Nationwide break-fix coverage means one call resolves infrastructure issues at any Oak Street location. No tracking down local contractors or explaining systems to unfamiliar technicians.

The Results

Infrastructure That Enables Growth

Accelerated Openings

Standardized processes and a dedicated team mean infrastructure never holds up center openings.

Consistent Patient Experience

Same infrastructure at every location supports the same care delivery model and patient experience.

Simplified IT Operations

Identical infrastructure across centers enables remote troubleshooting and reduces on-site support needs.

Single Vendor Relationship

One contract covers all centers—no managing multiple vendor relationships as the network expands.

Reliable Ongoing Support

Break-fix issues resolved quickly by technicians who know Oak Street's systems.

Future-Ready Partnership

As Oak Street enters new markets, Axseter is already there with coverage across all 50 states.
