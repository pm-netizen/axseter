Arthur J. Gallagher & Co.

M&A infrastructure standardization for the world's third-largest insurance broker.

Industry

Insurance Brokerage

The Challenge

Arthur J. Gallagher, the world's third-largest insurance broker, completed its $13.5 billion acquisition of AssuredPartners in August 2025—adding 250+ office locations across 38 states to its existing footprint. Each acquired location comes with its own legacy infrastructure: different cabling standards, access control systems, and IT configurations installed by dozens of regional contractors over the years.

The Solution

For companies executing aggressive M&A strategies, Axseter provides the infrastructure standardization backbone. One partner to assess, remediate, and bring acquired locations up to corporate standards—across structured cabling, video surveillance, and access control. No need to coordinate dozens of regional vendors. One contract, one point of accountability, one standard.

Scope of Work

  • Infrastructure assessments for acquired locations
  • Structured cabling remediation and standardization
  • Access control system upgrades and integration
  • Network infrastructure documentation
  • Ongoing break-fix support across locations
"

When you're integrating hundreds of acquired locations, you need a partner who can execute consistently at national scale. That's exactly what Axseter delivers.

The Result

Gallagher gained a single point of contact for infrastructure standardization across their entire M&A integration program. Instead of managing dozens of regional contractors with varying quality and responsiveness, they work with one partner who understands their standards and can execute anywhere in the country.
