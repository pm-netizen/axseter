Healthcare Solutions

Infrastructure That Supports Patient Care

Every healthcare location needs reliable, secure, and compliant infrastructure. Our services are designed specifically for the unique requirements of clinical environments.

Structured Cabling

High-performance Cat6A and fiber optic cabling for EMR systems, medical imaging, telehealth, and connected medical devices. Designed for the bandwidth demands of modern healthcare.

Access Control

Badge access, biometric systems, and visitor management to secure medication storage, patient areas, and sensitive equipment. Integration with your existing security platforms.

Video Surveillance

IP camera systems for patient safety, pharmacy security, and facility monitoring. Cloud and on-premise options with appropriate retention policies.

Sound Masking

Speech privacy systems for open floor plans, exam rooms, and consultation areas. Essential for HIPAA compliance in modern clinic designs.
